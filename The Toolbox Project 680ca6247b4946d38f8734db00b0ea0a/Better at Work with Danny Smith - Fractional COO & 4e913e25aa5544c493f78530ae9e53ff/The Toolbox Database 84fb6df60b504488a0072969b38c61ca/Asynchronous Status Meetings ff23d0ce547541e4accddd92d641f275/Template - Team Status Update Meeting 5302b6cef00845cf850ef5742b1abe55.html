Updates

Team status update:

This section is reserved for important updates about your team. This would include a greeting message for everyone, success celebrations, update on incoming hires, welcome to the new people that joined, update on current job openings and all other announcements that have impact on the team itself.

Projects & Tasks:

This section is reserved for updates regarding your ongoing projects, tasks or any other form of work that the team is involved in.

Health Check Report:

This section is reserved for a visual representation of the health grade for your ongoing work. This can be an update on your OKR projects, Health Check of the clients or anything else that could fall under this category for your team.

Processes and Tools:

This section is reserved for updates of your processes or new features of tools you are using to perform your core work in. Including short show-and-tell videos or quick tutorials to showcase these updates is very beneficial.

Spotlight:

This section is reserved for one of your team members to present something to the team- this could be something that was learned, or a success story that needs to be shared!

Guest star:

This section is reserved for a guest presenter from another team. Cross-team flow of information is very valuable and this is the place where they can share some knowledge or present something their team is working on and has value to your team.

Can I ask a few favours, please?

  • Requests and Questions
    • Don't be scared to hit me up about anything. I'm here to help.
    • If you ask me to do something it will get added to my list. If it's urgent, has a deadline or is blocking your work then please make that really clear when you ask so I can prioritise accordingly.
  • Slack
    • Prefer slack to email for most things. Especially short things. Default to posting in the open and @tagging me, rather than DM.
  • Email
    • Although I check emails regularly, I only deal with them in batches. I'll try to reply to important/urgent email within 24 hours, but will often "batch process" the rest once a week. I will eventually respond to all emails that seem to need one. If you need a quick response, say so in the email.
  • Meetings
    • If you fancy a chat, just organise a no-agenda zoom call - not every conversation needs a purpose.
    • If you invite me to a meeting with a specific purpose, I'd ❤️ it if could try to answer these questions in the invite:
      • Is there an agenda?
      • What's the desired outcome?
      • For group meetings where it's non-obvious... why am I being invited?
